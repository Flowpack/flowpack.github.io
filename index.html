Flowpack

Flow packages by a pack of Flow coders

This is an incubator and repository for high quality TYPO3 Flow packages that proved useful during development of Flow projects.

Single Sign-on

With the Flowpack.SingleSignOn.Server and Flowpack.SingleSignOn.Client packages we offer a full featured single sign-on solution between multiple TYPO3 Flow applications.
